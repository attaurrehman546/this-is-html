Use of font color

The use of font color plays a significant role in enhancing the overall appearance of your document. Choosing the right font color can make your text more visually appealing and help emphasize important points, making the content easier to read and more engaging for your audience. Proper use of color also contributes to the mood and tone of the document, supporting the message you want to convey.

Additionally, carefully selected font colors improve accessibility by ensuring sufficient contrast with the background, which is essential for readability, especially for people with visual impairments. Therefore, thoughtful use of font color not only beautifies your document but also improves its effectiveness and user experience.and user frindly.
