Use of alignment

In this lesson, I will teach you how to align your text or elements on a web page using HTML and CSS. You can align content to the left, center, or right side of the page using simple style properties.

By default, most elements like paragraphs and headings are aligned to the left. But with the style="text-align:..." property, you can change the alignment to make your content look more organized or visually appealing.

For example, you might want to center a heading to make it stand out, or align a paragraph to the right to create a unique layout. Let’s learn how to do that step by step with examples.
